Get Started with SubSense

Free Tier Features

  • Track up to 5 subscriptions
  • Basic analytics: total spend & next billing date
  • Category filtering & search
  • Standard renewal reminders
  • Local data storage & manual iCloud backup

Premium Tier Features

  • Unlimited subscriptions
  • Advanced analytics & cost optimization
  • Personalized recommendations to save money
  • Export to CSV/PDF & custom themes/icons
  • Family sharing & priority customer support

Subscription Plans

Choose monthly or annual billing (annual plan includes optional free trial and discounted rate).

How to Add Your First Subscription

Tap “Add Subscription”, fill in the name, billing date, and cost, then save to start tracking.
